Distribution with unbounded support

Distribution with two peaks.

x = [0.1*randn(50,1); randn(50,1)+6];
gkdeb(x);

Distribution with upper and lower bounds: normal distribution

clear
x=rand(1000,1);
% PDF with bounded support
p.lB=0;
p.uB=1;
fhandle=figure;
gkdeb(x,p);
% Compare with unbounded PDF estimate
p=gkdeb(x);
figure(fhandle)
subplot(211)
hold
plot(p.x,p.f,'r--','linewidth',2);
axis([-0.5 1.5 0 1.1])
legend('Bounded','Unbounded','Location','South')
subplot(212)
hold
plot(p.x,p.F,'r--','linewidth',2);
axis([-0.5 1.5 0 1.1])
legend('Bounded','Unbounded','Location','SouthEast')
